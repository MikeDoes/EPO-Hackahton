Universal Patent Classifier

By Team Patent Variable

Introduction

As part of PatentVariable's submission for the EPO's Codefest on Green Plastics Hackathon, we developed an interactive demonstration for our Universal Patent Classifier. This interactive demo allows users to quickly and accurately match patents to their respective CPCs. To enhance the quality of the application in a qualitative manner, we also provide three potential reasons for why a patent is classified in a given CPC.

From a technical standpoint, we achieved this performance through fine-tuning a state-of-the-art GPT-3 model with claims extracted from the EPO's Open Data Services API. This interactive dashboard is available free of charge through the credentials provided in the presentation. If you have any issues with the credentials, please e-mail mike@ipft.co
